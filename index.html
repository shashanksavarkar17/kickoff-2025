General Rules

  1. Team Composition: 2 to 5 members, with at least 1 female member required per team.
  2. Robot Dimensions: Must fit within a 35CM x 25CM x 15CM (LWH) cube at the start of a match.
  3. Robot Weight: Maximum weight allowed is 2 kg.
  4. Power Source: Robots must be powered by batteries with a maximum voltage of 24V DC.
  5. Control System: Robots must be remotely controlled, though autonomous functions are permitted.
  6. Match Format: Matches consist of two 5-minute halves with a 1-minute break. The tournament follows a "Double-Elimination" method.
  7. Scoring: A goal is worth 1 point. Ties are broken by a 3-minute sudden death overtime, followed by a penalty shootout if necessary.
  8. Safety & Conduct: While robot interaction is allowed, intentional damage is strictly prohibited and unsportsmanlike conduct can lead to disqualification.
