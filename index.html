General Rules

  1. Team: 2 to 4 students.
  2. Robot dimensions: (l × b × h) ≤ (35cm × 25cm × 15cm), ≤ 2kg weight.
  3. Autonomy: Manual control (wired or wireless)... (More in Official Rulebook)
  4. Batteries: Onboard, ≤ 24V DC. Teams must provide inspection sheet.
  5. Match format: Knockout, one-on-one matches.
  6. Scoring: Goal = 50 points. Own goals count for opponent. Referee decisions final.
  7. Safety: Bots must have protective sheets for safety.
  8. Penalties: Unsportsmanlike conduct/sabotage = penalties/disqualification.
